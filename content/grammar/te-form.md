# 〜て form

**JLPT level:** N5

Connects verbs/actions in sequence, used for requests (て+ください), and progressive/state forms (て+いる).

## Example
- **JP:** 朝ご飯を食べて、会社に行きます。
- **Reading:** asagohan o tabete, kaisha ni ikimasu.
- **EN:** I eat breakfast and then go to work.
