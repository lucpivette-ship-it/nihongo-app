# 敬語 — honorific & humble speech

**JLPT level:** N3

Sonkeigo (尊敬語) elevates the listener/other party; kenjougo (謙譲語) humbles the speaker's own actions. Essential for Japanese business settings.

## Example
- **JP:** 部長がいらっしゃいました。
- **Reading:** buchou ga irasshaimashita.
- **EN:** The department head has arrived. (honorific for 'came')
