# 私の一日 — My Day

**JLPT level:** N5

## Passage
私は毎朝七時に起きます。朝ご飯を食べて、電車で会社に行きます。会社は九時から始まります。仕事の後、友達と晩ご飯を食べます。夜は本を読んで、十一時に寝ます。

## Reading
watashi wa maiasa shichi-ji ni okimasu. asagohan o tabete, densha de kaisha ni ikimasu. kaisha wa ku-ji kara hajimarimasu. shigoto no ato, tomodachi to bangohan o tabemasu. yoru wa hon o yonde, juuichi-ji ni nemasu.

## Translation
I wake up at seven every morning. I eat breakfast, then go to the office by train. Work starts at nine. After work, I have dinner with a friend. At night I read a book, and go to bed at eleven.

## Comprehension questions
- What time does the writer wake up?
- How does the writer get to work?
- What does the writer do before going to bed?
