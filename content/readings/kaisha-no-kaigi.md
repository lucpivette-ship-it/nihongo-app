# 会社の会議 — A Meeting at Work

**JLPT level:** N4

## Passage
今日は午後二時から会議があります。会議室で新しいプロジェクトについて話します。資料はメールで送りました。会議の後、報告書を書かなければなりません。

## Reading
kyou wa gogo ni-ji kara kaigi ga arimasu. kaigishitsu de atarashii purojekuto ni tsuite hanashimasu. shiryou wa meeru de okurimashita. kaigi no ato, houkokusho o kakanakereba narimasen.

## Translation
Today there's a meeting starting at 2pm. We'll discuss the new project in the meeting room. I sent the materials by email. After the meeting, I have to write a report.

## Comprehension questions
- What time does the meeting start?
- How were the materials sent?
- What must be done after the meeting?
