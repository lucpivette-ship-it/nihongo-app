# 〜ば — conditional

**JLPT level:** N3

A more formal conditional form, often used in written or business Japanese.

## Example
- **JP:** 早く出発すれば、間に合います。
- **Reading:** hayaku shuppatsu sureba, maniaimasu.
- **EN:** If we leave early, we'll make it in time.
